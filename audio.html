FIG. 04 — Audio / DJ

Sequence and signal study.

DJ sets and mix structures treated as human-systems research: pacing, tension, feedback, release, and the emotional ergonomics of a room. Every set is a live test of a hypothesis about what a crowd needs next.

Approach

Behavioral research, 2024–present

  1. 01

    Pacing

    Reading energy in real time and deciding, track to track, whether to build, hold, or reset.

  2. 02

    Tension & release

    Structuring a set the way a good story is structured — earn the peak before you play it.

  3. 03

    Feedback

    Treating a crowd like a live dataset — what people actually respond to over what's supposed to work.
